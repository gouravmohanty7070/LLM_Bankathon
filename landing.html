Features

Our ultimate goal is to optimize HR operations, minimize manual efforts, enhance decision-making, and improve the overall employee experience.

Job Description Evaluation

Optimize your job postings with our LLM-based application. We analyze job descriptions and provide enhancement recommendations based on the job title. Choose to implement suggested changes for attracting top talent effortlessly.

CV Ranking and Candidate Shortlisting

Simplify recruitment with our CV ranking feature. We rank CVs based on job requirements, enabling quick identification of the best-fit candidates. Get additional insights for informed hiring decisions.

Tailored Screening Questions

Streamline interviews with tailored screening questions. Evaluate candidate responses and make way for successful in-person rounds.
